Research

My interests revolve around the convergence of machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, with a focus on gaining insights into human cognition. I am enthusiastic about exploring language grounding within multimodal contexts and investigating the linguistic and cognitive characteristics of these models. Representative papers are highlighted.
